Who We Are

McAllister, Inc. is a full service site work and site utillity business that is family owned and operated by Kenneth McAllister and his son Chris McAllister.


McAllister, Inc. was founded in 1973 and we have been servicing Northwest Indiana and the Greater Chicagoland area for over 40 years! The picture shown is our main location in Cedar Lake, IN.


McAllister, Inc. has formal trade agreements with with Engineers Opereating Local 150, Laborers Local 81, and Teamsters Local 142. Our skilled tradesmen are up-to date on industry safety measures and are continually educated.


Our helpful and supportive staff is just a phone call away! You can reach us at (219) 374-9498 for a FREE estimate!

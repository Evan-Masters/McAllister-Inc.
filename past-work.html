Past Work

Hanover Community Schools

Hanover Community Schools

McAllister, Inc. has completed multiple projects for Hanover Community Schools such as site work and site utilities for various campuses. For Hanover Central High School we did the site work and site utilities for the indoor field house, main gym, football field, soccer field, softball Field, baseball field, tennis courts as well as directional boring, retention ponds, parking lots, and an addition to the cafeteria. For the Hanover Central Middle School, we did directional boring and grading. We have also done work for both elementary schools. For Jane Ball Elementary School, we did parking lot, water main, storm sewer utilities, retention pond, and building additions.
